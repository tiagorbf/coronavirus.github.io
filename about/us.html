About Corona Plots

The corona virus evolved very fast leaving a lot of people, business and governments clueless about how to react.

Fortunetly there are a lot of good people making trustworthy and up to date COVID-19 data available and a lot of interesting and well thought articles.

My personal goal with this project is to contribute to the comunity with easy to use dashboards that will empower the curious minds to dive deeper in the data and to take their own conclusions based on facts.
